Our Story

A journey of passion and tradition

Meet Aunty Ji

Founded in 2022, Ajanta Ovens is a beloved neighborhood bakery run by an elderly woman known affectionately as "Aunty Ji." Operating from her small home kitchen in Saharanpur, Uttar Pradesh, she crafts traditional, handcrafted baked goods that have won the hearts of locals.

A widowed grandmother in her late 70s, Aunty Ji launched Ajanta Ovens to share her lifelong passion for baking. Using recipes passed down through generations and her signature wood-fired oven, she started baking for neighbors, turning her modest venture into a local favorite through her dedication and flavorful creations.
